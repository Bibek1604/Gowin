Our Story

Founded in 2009 in the heart of Kathmandu, Nepal Adventures began with a simple mission: to share the breathtaking beauty and rich culture of Nepal with travelers from around the world. What started as a small team of passionate mountaineers has grown into one of Nepal's most trusted travel agencies.

Based in Shankhamul, Kathmandu, we specialize in creating unforgettable experiences - from challenging Himalayan treks to serene cultural tours and thrilling wildlife safaris. Our deep connection to the land, combined with years of expertise, ensures every journey is safe, authentic, and truly memorable.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional travel experiences that showcase the natural beauty, cultural richness, and warm hospitality of Nepal while ensuring the highest standards of safety, sustainability, and customer satisfaction. We strive to create journeys that inspire, educate, and transform our travelers.

Our Vision

To be recognized as Nepal's leading travel agency, setting the benchmark for quality, innovation, and responsible tourism. We envision a future where every traveler experiences the magic of Nepal through authentic, sustainable, and life-enriching adventures that benefit local communities and preserve our natural heritage.
